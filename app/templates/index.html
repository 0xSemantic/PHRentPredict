Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about PHRentPredict.

How accurate are the predictions?

Our models aim for a Mean Absolute Percentage Error (MAPE) of less than 15%. However, rent markets can be volatile, so predictions should be used as guidance rather than absolute certainty.

What factors do you consider in your forecasts?

We analyze historical rent data, economic indicators (inflation, oil prices), demographic factors (population influx), utility costs (NEPA bills), and local housing dynamics.

Do you include agent fees and other costs?

Yes! We provide a complete cost breakdown including agent fees (15-30%), caution deposits (1-2 months rent), and other compulsory fees that can significantly increase your move-in costs.

Is PHRentPredict completely free?

Yes! PHRentPredict is completely free and open to everyone. No registration or payment is required to use our forecasting tools.
